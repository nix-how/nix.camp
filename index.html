Nix Camp

Nix Camp 2023 is a week long event from September 24th to October 1st. A time to come together and learn about Nix whilst living near to the Welsh/British countryside, surrounded by nature and friends. There are no formal talks, training sessions or workshops; though attendees are encouraged to give informal talks to eachother. A week of living, breathing, learning and sometimes sleeping with Nix.

This is the third Nix Camp.

Tickets/Booking

The event is fully booked, and no more applications can be taken, but don't worry, the next camp will be even better.

The Nix Camp dates are September 24th until October 1st. Tickets are priced at £200 per person. This covers accomodation and attendance.

Nix Camp is a 1 week long event. Depending on demand, this could increase to two weeks.

Since this third Nix Camp is expected to be close-knit, there is no automated booking system, we will be manually accounting and replying to individuals via email.

Venue & Accommodation

Accommodation will be provided by the BOARC, an artistic research center that promotes creativity, collaboration and innovation located in Birkenhead, Wirral, Merseyside, England. Wilding Way, Birkenhead, Prenton CH43 7RA Practical information about how to arrive at the venue and more can be found at https://bidstonobservatory.org/practical-info

Bidston Observatory Artistic Research Centre (BOARC) is a self-organising, communal study site. It is a not-for-profit project that focuses on providing individual users and groups with a low cost, temporary place to work and stay together; to develop projects that require time and space in a flexible, non-pressured environment.

FAQ

  • Is this event sponsored? - We have been granted funding from the NixOS Foundation that has not yet resolved via OpenCollective!. We have not yet recieved any other interest in corporate sponsorship. If you would like to sponsor the camp, please email us at camp@nix.how.
