Nix Camp (unofficial)

Nix Camp is a month long event from August 2nd to October 15th. A time to come together and learn about Nix whilst living in the midst of Wales, Snowdonia, surrounded by nature. There are no formal talks or workshops. A month of living, breathing, learning and sometimes sleeping with Nix.

This first ever Nix Camp we will be prioritising tickets to those enrolled in the Summer of Nix (SoN) program. However if you still want to attend and are a Nix enthusiast there is still the possibility of attendance.

Tickets/Booking

Tickets are priced at £30 per person. This covers attendance and food. Accomodation is a separate expense that is discussed below in Venue & Accommodation

Nix Camp is split into 4, week long sessions between the dates of August 2nd to October 15th. You can sign up for a given week, or stay for the whole month if you so desire.

Since this first Nix Camp is expected to be close-knit, there is no automated booking system, we will be manually accounting.

If you would like to participate in the first ever Nix Camp, please send us an email with the subject "Attending Nix Camp" at camp@nix.how with answers to the following questions:

  • What is your name? (Aliases are also accepted)
  • What weeks you would like to attend? (1, 2, 3, 4)
  • Are part of the Summer of Nix program?
  • Will you find accommodation of your own?
  • What is your preferred payment method? (Bank Transfer, Paypal, Cryptocurrency)

Venue & Accommodation

Accommodation will be provided by the Astralship, a hackerspace that promotes creativity, collbaration and innovation located in Deiniolen, Wales. Cefn Y Waun, Festri, Astral Road, Deiniolen, Caernarfon LL55 3NB

The Astralship is based in a 200 year old chapel, where the mountains of Snowdonia meet the sea. The chapel dates from 1838 and is located in an unbelievably beautiful area, with endless mountains, beaches and quarries to explore. It's the perfect place to be if you like hiking, climbing and wild swimming.

Food and accommodation is offered on-site for the duration of the event at the cost of £40/night. There are many AirBNB and hotel offerings available in the surrounding area if this arrangement does not suit you.
